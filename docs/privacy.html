Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains that the N4YuC4 Personal Website is a static website and does not collect, store, or process any personal data from its visitors.

1. No Data Collection

Our website is designed as a static informational site. We do not use contact forms, user accounts, or any other methods to collect personal information (such as names, email addresses, or phone numbers) directly from you.

2. No Tracking or Cookies

  • Cookies: We do not use cookies to track your behavior or store preferences on our website.
  • Analytics: We do not use Google Analytics or any other third-party tracking services to monitor visitor activity.
  • Server Logs: While the web server hosting this site (e.g., GitHub Pages) may log basic technical information (like IP addresses) for security and maintenance purposes, the website owner does not have access to personally identifiable information from these logs and does not use them for tracking.

3. Third-Party Links

Our website contains links to third-party platforms (such as Medium, LinkedIn, and GitHub). Once you click these links and leave our site, we have no control over the privacy practices of those websites. We recommend reviewing the privacy policies of any third-party site you visit.

4. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy if the website's functionality changes in the future.

5. Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy statement, you can reach out via the contact methods listed on the Contact page.
